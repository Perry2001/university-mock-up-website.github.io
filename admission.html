Courses We offer

Computer Science

Learn the principles of computer programming, data structures, algorithms, and software engineering. Study programming languages like C++, Java and Python, and gain hands-on experience with projects and labs.

Business Management

Study the fundamentals of accounting, finance, marketing, and operations management. Learn how to analyze data and make strategic business decisions. Explore the latest trends and best practices in the field through case studies and simulations.

History

Study the past and its impact on the present and future. Learn about different cultures, societies, and civilizations through the ages. Analyze primary sources and historical texts to gain a deeper understanding of historical events and their significance.

Biology

Explore the complexity and diversity of living organisms. Learn about genetics, evolution, ecology, and psychiatry. Gain hands-on experience with experiments and lab work. Study the latest research and developments in the field.
